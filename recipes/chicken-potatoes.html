Chicken with potatoes in the oven

Description

This food is quite common in greek households and quite easy to pick up for people that just started to live on their own. Simple enough and a doesn't too much time to prepare.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast and Wings in pieces
  • Potatoes
  • Mustard

    Steps

  1. Put the chicken pieces in a bowl and put the Mustard and spices of choice and mix em up
  2. Slice the potatoes either like fries sized or cubes and season them
  3. Then put them in a tray, add a lil water and cover it with a parchment paper
  4. Put it in the oven to bake for 90minutes at 180o
