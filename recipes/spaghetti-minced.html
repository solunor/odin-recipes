Spaghetti with Minced Meat

Description

This one of the most default but also favorite foods you'll find in a greek household. It's simple quite charming, quick to make and it's also not too expensive either. Pretty much anyone can make it and it doesn't require much knowledge in cooking either.

    Incredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Beef/Pork Meat
  • Chopped Onion Pieces
  • Tomato Sauce

    Steps

  1. You cook the chopped onion pieces for a while in a small cooking pot
  2. After a while you added the minced meat
  3. After the minced meat has taken some colour, you also add the sauce and some water
  4. You let it cook for another 15-20 minutes and add water 1-2 times depending if it drains too much water
  5. Once you are at the cooking phase of the minced meat you put a large cooking pot with water to heat
  6. Once it starts to boiling you put the spaghetti in
  7. You mix every 1 to 2 minutes so the spaghetti doesn't get stuck in the bottom
  8. Boil for the recommended time the package says.
  9. Rince and serve
