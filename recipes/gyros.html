Pita Gyros (A Greek Favorite)

Description

Pita gyros is one of the most common traditional greek fast foods you are gonna encounter. Especially in the bigger cities you are gonna encounter shops selling it a lot. It's indistinguished combination of different flavors, makes it one of the best fast foods out there, that many across the world know it and many tourists are seeking it upon their visit in Greece.

Ingredients (Common Variant)

  • Chopped Pork Pieces
  • Pita Bread
  • Tzatziki
  • Sliced Onion Pieces
  • Parsley
  • French Fries

    Steps

  1. Pita Bread
  2. Tzatziki

  4. Sliced Onion Pieces
